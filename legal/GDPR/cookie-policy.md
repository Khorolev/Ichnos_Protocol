## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.
They help the website function, remember your preferences, and provide
analytics data.

## Your Choices

When you first visit our website, a cookie banner will ask for your consent
to optional cookies. You can:

- **Accept all**: Enable analytics cookies
- **Reject all**: Only strictly necessary cookies will be used
- **Manage preferences**: Choose which optional cookie categories to enable

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings"
link in the website footer.
