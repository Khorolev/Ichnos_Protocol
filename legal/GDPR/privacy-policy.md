## 9. Account Deletion and Anonymization

When you request account deletion:

1. Your account is deactivated immediately
2. Your contact details (name, email, company, phone, LinkedIn) are removed
   from all records
3. Your chat questions and inquiry content are retained in anonymized form —
   your identity is removed but the question text is kept for aggregated
   analytics about market demand
4. You are logged out and cannot log back in
5. This process is irreversible
