## 1. Purpose of Processing

We store and analyze questions submitted through our AI chat assistant to:

- Understand what topics potential customers care about (market demand
  intelligence)
- Identify frequently asked questions to improve our AI assistant responses
- Track inquiry trends over time to inform product development priorities
- Generate aggregated topic analytics for internal business reporting

---

## 2. Identify the Legitimate Interest

Our legitimate interest is the improvement and operation of our business
services. Specifically:

- Understanding customer needs is essential for a startup entering the
  battery passport compliance market
- Analyzing chat questions allows us to refine our AI assistant to provide
  better answers
- Aggregated topic data informs which product features and services to
  prioritize
- This processing is a normal and expected part of operating a B2B service
  with an AI chat feature

---

## 3. Necessity Test

Is this processing necessary for the stated purpose? Could the purpose be
achieved in a less intrusive way?

- Without storing chat data, we would have no record of what potential
  customers ask, making it impossible to improve the AI or understand market
  demand
- Anonymous survey data would not capture the specificity and volume of
  real-time questions
- Aggregating data without any per-question storage would prevent us from
  improving AI responses to specific question types
- We minimize data by not including user names, emails, or other PII in the
  analytics processing — only question text, AI responses, and derived
  topic labels

Conclusion: The processing is necessary. Less intrusive alternatives would
not achieve the business purpose.

## 4. Balancing Test

### Interests of the Data Controller

- Understanding market demand for a new product category
- Improving AI assistant quality
- Informing product development roadmap
- Standard business analytics practice in B2B SaaS

### Rights and Interests of the Data Subject

- Right to privacy in their communications
- Reasonable expectation about how their questions are used
- Potential concern about being profiled or targeted

| Nature of data          | Professional/commercial questions about battery regulations, not sensitive personal data           |
| Data subjects           | Business professionals acting in a professional capacity                                           |
| Relationship            | Data subjects have voluntarily engaged with our chat assistant                                     |
| Reasonable expectations | Users of a B2B chat tool would reasonably expect their questions to be used to improve the service |

### Conclusion

Our legitimate interest in analyzing chat data does not override the rights
and freedoms of the data subjects. The processing involves low-sensitivity
commercial data from professionals who have voluntarily engaged with our
service, and robust safeguards are in place.

## 5. Safeguards

We apply the following measures to protect data subjects:

1. **Transparency**: Our Privacy Policy clearly states that chat data is
   processed under legitimate interest and explains the purpose
2. **Notice at point of collection**: A visible notice appears before the
   chat begins, informing users their questions are stored
3. **Right to object**: Users can email us to object to chat analytics
   processing; we will anonymize their records within 30 days
4. **Data minimization**: We do not send user PII (name, email) to the AI
   provider — only question text. Analytics processing uses question content
   and topic labels, not user identity
5. **Retention limits**: Chat data is retained for 36 months, then
   anonymized or deleted
6. **Anonymization on deletion**: When a user requests account deletion,
   their chat records are anonymized (identity removed) while retaining
   question text for aggregate analytics only
7. **No profiling or automated decisions**: Chat analytics are used for
   aggregate business intelligence, not to profile or make decisions about
   individual users
8. **Access controls**: Chat data is accessible only to authorized personnel
   (admin role) via authenticated admin dashboard
9. **Security**: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with parameterized
   queries preventing injection attacks

Based on this assessment, we conclude that processing chat analytics data
