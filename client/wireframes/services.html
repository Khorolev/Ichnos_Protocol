Our Services

Expert consulting and development services for companies navigating the battery regulation landscape

Ichnos Protocol combines deep battery engineering expertise with regulatory knowledge and software development capability to deliver pragmatic solutions designed for implementation, not just documentation.

Battery Regulation Compliance

Navigate the EU Battery Regulation with confidence. We provide gap analysis, compliance roadmaps, and documentation preparation to ensure your products meet all regulatory requirements.

  • Comprehensive gap analysis against EU Battery Regulation requirements
  • Compliance roadmap development with clear milestones
  • Technical documentation preparation for regulatory submissions
  • Ongoing regulatory monitoring and updates

Homologation Support

End-to-end support for type approval processes, including coordination with notified bodies and testing centers across the EU.

  • Type approval process management (UN ECE R100, GTR No. 20)
  • Coordination with notified bodies and certification authorities
  • Technical file preparation and submission
  • Compliance verification and audit support

Testing Center Coordination

Selection of and liaison with accredited testing centers for battery safety, performance, and durability assessments.

  • Testing center selection based on requirements and budget
  • Test plan development and review
  • Coordination of testing schedules and logistics
  • Results analysis and reporting

Requirement Management

Structure and manage technical, legal, and regulatory requirements for battery system development with systematic traceability.

  • Requirements elicitation and documentation
  • Traceability matrix development
  • Change management and version control
  • Stakeholder alignment and communication

Battery Passport Development (In-House)

Help your engineering team build a Battery Passport solution tailored to your products and processes, with hands-on technical guidance.

  • Data model design aligned with DIN SPEC 99100
  • System architecture and technology selection
  • Integration with existing PLM/ERP systems
  • Implementation support and code review

Circular Economy Strategy

Advisory on remanufacturing, repurposing, and second-life strategies to maximize battery asset value and enable sustainable business models.

  • Remanufacturing feasibility assessment
  • Second-life application identification and matching
  • Reverse logistics and collection strategy
  • Business model development for circular operations
