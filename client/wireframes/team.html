Dr.-Ing. Francesco Maltoni

Founder & Lead Consultant

Dr.-Ing. Maltoni founded Ichnos Protocol after a career spanning research and industry leadership in the battery and automotive sectors. His unique combination of academic rigor, industry experience, and software development skills positions Ichnos Protocol to deliver both regulatory compliance and technical innovation.

With a doctorate from RWTH Aachen University focused on circular economy for battery systems and remanufacturing, combined with hands-on leadership as Lead Expert for Battery Systems at FEV Europe, Dr.-Ing. Maltoni brings rare depth to battery consulting.

His full-stack web development training at Sigma School enables Ichnos Protocol to build the Battery Passport solution in-house, ensuring deep integration between regulatory requirements and technical implementation.
